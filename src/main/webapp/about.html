About Tech Symposium 2026

Tech Symposium 2026 is the flagship annual technical event organized by our college. The symposium brings together students, faculty members, researchers and industry experts from various institutions to promote innovation, collaboration and technical excellence.

🎯 Our Vision

To inspire students through innovation, technology and collaborative learning.

🚀 Our Mission

Provide a platform for students to showcase innovative ideas, participate in technical competitions and collaborate with industry professionals.

🏆 Objectives

Encourage research, teamwork, leadership and practical implementation of emerging technologies.
